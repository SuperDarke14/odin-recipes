Artisan Bread

Description

This bread recipe comes from the New York Times. It's really easy and very tasty. I've made it plenty of times before.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of flour, plus a bit more for surfaces
  • 1 1/2 cups of water
  • 1 tsp. yeast
  • 2 tsp. salt
  • Cornmeal (optional, but recommended)

Directions

  1. Mix yeast, salt, flour and water until just mixed. It should be a bit shaggier than normal bread dough
  2. Cover dough and let rise overnight
  3. Turn dough out of bowl and fold it over itself horizontally and vertically
  4. Sprinkle cornmeal over the dough
  5. Preheat oven to 500F with a Dutch oven inside
  6. Once oven comes to temperature, take out Dutch oven and place dough into Dutch oven.
  7. Replace Dutch oven and bake at 500F for 30 minutes; remove lid and bake for 15 minutes more

Prep time: 10+ hours.

Bake time: 45 minutes.
