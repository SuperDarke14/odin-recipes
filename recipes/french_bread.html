French Bread

Description

This bread started me on my baking journey. I've made it plenty of times before.

Ingredients

  • 5 1/2-6 cups of flour, plus a bit more for surfaces
  • 3 cups of water
  • 2 tsp. salt
  • 1 packet (3 tbsp.) yeast
  • Cornmeal (optional, but recommended)

Directions

  1. Mix yeast, salt, flour and water until just mixed.
  2. Knead for 10 minutes.
  3. Cover dough and let rise for 2 hours.
  4. Punch dough down, divide in half, turn onto floured surface and roll into long loaves (1 foot would be a good length)
  5. Cover and let rise another 45 minutes.
  6. Preheat oven to 375F
  7. Once oven comes to temperature, score loaves and put on a baking sheet with parchment.
  8. Bake for 30 minutes.

Prep time: 3+ hours.

Bake time: 35 minutes.
